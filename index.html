Introduction

Welcome! My name is Bijan Oviedo, I'm 21 years old and am currently in my third year studying Computer Science at the University of Chicago.

What do I do? I code. I started programming in BASIC on a TI-82 calculator when I was about 12 years old. I wrote programs that did things like print my name 100 times. I thought to myself: this thing has potential.

I love writing high-performance, multi-threaded code, so my weapon of choice for problem-solving is C/C++. But whether it's low level or high level, I love tinkering with new technologies, and can often be found making websites with HTML/CSS/JavaScript, learning iOS and Android development, or studying machine learning with Python.

When I'm not coding, I enjoy playing the trumpet, reading about law, and traveling with my family.
